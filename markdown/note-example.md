## Section with Custom Anchor (Optional) <a id="custom-section"></a>

Sometimes you may want a **stable or cleaner link** than the auto-generated one.

Example:
- Auto-generated might be: `#section-with-custom-anchor`
- But you define: `#custom-section`

Link:
[Jump to Section with Custom Anchor](#custom-section)

This is useful when:
- Heading names may change, but links should stay stable  
- You want shorter or more readable URLs  
- Your platform generates inconsistent anchors

## Important Note with custom anchor (Optional)

Custom anchors are also useful inside content:

<a id="important-note"></a>
**Important Note:** This paragraph explains something critical that you may want to reference directly.

Link:
[Jump to Important Note](#important-note)

This is useful when:
- You want to link to a **specific paragraph**, not a whole section  
- You need **multiple anchor points inside one section**
